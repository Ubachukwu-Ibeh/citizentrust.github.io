About Us

Citizen Trust Limited is a Venture Capital/Private Equity Firm in Broad Street Lagos Island Nigeria with interest in seed, early-stage, growth investments into startups and existing firms that possess high and stable growth potentials with visions that align with our Strategic Focus as well as our clients' goals and objectives.


Founders see us as an interestingly amazing solutionist, as we are unrestricted and unbounded by traditional industry limitations and practices. At Citizen Trust, we create a fine blend of partnership, mentorship and adequate funding for our investors and investees alike such that the integration is seamless even as we maintain multi-diversification of cultures, races, beliefs with strong ethical stance and balance.


Citizen Trust is a trademark and a duly registered Venture Capital Fund Management Firm with the Corporate Affairs Commission(CAC) and the Securities And Exchange Commission(SEC) in the Federal Republic of Nigeria.

Abayomi Michael Osho

Managing Director/CEO

Abayomi Michael Osho

Managing Director/CEO

Abayomi is a seasoned Entrepreneur, Banker, Trainer and Strategy Consultant, with over a decade of distinctive professional experience spanning Auditing, Leasing, consulting and Microfinance Banking. He obtained his BSc degree in Economics from the Ogun State University now Olabisi Onabanjo University and an MSc degree in Strategic Planning from Edinburgh Business School, Heriot-Watt University Scotland United Kingdom. He is a Certified Member of the Chartered Institute of Bankers of Nigeria(CIBN) A Fellow of the Association of Enterprise Risk Management Professionals of Nigeria(FERP), an Associate of the Institute of Chartered Economists of Nigeria(ACE), a Certified Member of the Financial Reporting Council of Nigeria(FRC). He is also a Certified Consultant in Project & Change Management, Mergers & Acquisitions, Leadership, Strategic Negotiations, Competitive Strategy e.t.c. He started his professional career with Akin & Hughes Chartered Accountants where he gathered in-depth auditing skills working with different teams of experienced auditors with portfolios consisting Commercial Banks, Oil and Gas firms, manufacturing companies e.t.c. He is the founder and Pioneer Managing Director of Verite Microfinance Bank Ltd which under his leadership progressed tremendously. He is currently the Group Managing Director/CEO of Asset & Equity Group(AEG), an Investment Banking and Private Equity Group As a highly energetic and unapologetic investor into humanity, Abayomi founded the famous Financial Literacy For Youth Initiative(FLY-I) in 2010. An NGO that focuses on educating mostly teenagers and young adult members of the Nigerian population and equip them with adequate knowledge of finance sufficient to effectively run businesses and manage monetary and non-monetary assets. More than 50,000 youths have benefited from the activities of the organization
